About Buffr

Buffr is more than a payment or banking app. We're "Your Next Payment Companion," enhancing financial inclusion with modern, futuristic payments. Our innovative approach ensures financial services are accessible, affordable, and enjoyable.

Our Mission

Empowering users with personalized financial services and seamless, secure, innovative payment solutions.

Our Vision

To empower individuals and businesses in Southern Africa by making financial services accessible, affordable, and enjoyable.

Our Features

AI-Powered Financial Advisor

Unlock your financial potential with Buffr's intuitive AI-powered financial advisor. Get personalized budgeting and savings recommendations based on your data and financial goals. Make informed decisions and achieve financial well-being with ease.

Unified Dashboard

Manage multiple bank accounts and mobile money wallets in one place. Buffr provides a unified dashboard, gateway to accessible credit, and seamless payment experience across multiple financial platforms.

Innovative Payment Solutions

Make payments easy and enjoyable with Buffr. Gain and redeem Buffr Points for app engagement. Explore various offers, savings and budgeting tools, contactless payment options, and group payments. Experience a new way to manage your finances.

AI Credit Scoring

Build your credit score and access financial opportunities with Buffr. Our AI credit scoring engine leverages alternative data sources to assess creditworthiness, enabling individuals without traditional credit history to access credit. Experience fair and comprehensive assessment for everyone.

USSD & QR Code Payments

Buffr makes payments simple and accessible. Use USSD codes or scan QR codes to send and receive money instantly. Enjoy a convenient and secure payment experience, even without internet access.

Personalized Loans

Buffr offers personalized loan options tailored to your needs. Our AI-powered system analyzes your financial data to provide you with the best loan terms and rates. Access the financial support you need with ease and transparency.

Buffr Points Rewards

Earn Buffr Points for everyday transactions and app engagement. Redeem your points for exciting rewards, discounts, and exclusive offers. Make managing your finances fun and rewarding with Buffr.

Comprehensive Menu Management

Business Intelligence Tools

Make informed decisions with our powerful business intelligence tools. Track sales, analyze customer preferences, and gain insights into your restaurant's performance to drive growth and profitability.

User Profile Management

Manage customer profiles to offer personalized experiences. Keep track of customer preferences, dining history, and loyalty rewards, enhancing customer satisfaction and encouraging repeat business.
